Mitochondrial genomes for Ommatogammarus albinus, Ommatogammarus flavus and Ommatogammarus melanophthalmus were assembled via the same procedure from WGS reads available from BioProject PRJDB40454. Reads were quality- and adapter-trimmed with bbduk.sh from BBmap v38.95, then reads corresponding to the related mitochondrial genome of Eulimnogammarus cyaneus (KX341964) were selected with bbduk.sh. The filtered reads were de novo assembled and annotated with MitoFinder v1.4.2; KX341964 was provided as reference (-r) for finding mitochondrial contigs and annotation. The obtain contigs were used as baits for MITObim v1.9.1 to finish circular assemblies. At the MITObim stage, all quality-trimmed reads interleaved with bbduk.sh were used. The assemblies were annotated with MitoFinder once more. At  this stage, circular mitogenomes were found for all species. The assemblies were standardized using the mtgrasp_standardize.py script from Mtgrasp v1.1.8 and annotated with MitoFinder once more.
